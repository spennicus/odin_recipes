Braised Beef Short Ribs

Ingredients

  • 6-8 lbs beef short ribs, bone-in
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 3 celery stalks
  • 3 carrots, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 whole head of garlic
  • 2 cups dry red wine
  • 1 quart beef stock
  • 3 sprigs of thyme

Preparation

  1. Season the short ribs liberally with salt and pepper and sear on high heat in a large dutch oven. Remove from pot.
  2. Dice onion, carrots, and celery and add to the pot. Let cook on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes, until onion is translucent.
  3. Add tomato paste and stir into cooking mirepoix.
  4. Slice garlic head in half and place in middle of dutch oven, with direct contact on the pot. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add red wine and cook for 5 minutes, allowing alcohol to cook off entirely.
  6. Add beef stock and bring everything to a boil.
  7. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and add sprigs of thyme. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or longer, until short rib is tender and falls off the bone with little resistance.
  8. Remove short rib from pot and strain solids from cooking liquid. reduce cooking liquid until a sauce-like consistency.
  9. Season to taste and serve with mashed potatos, or another side you prefer!
