Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs potatoes
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 1.2 cup of sour cream
  • salt and fresh ground pepper

Preparation

  1. Boil potatoes in a large pot for 16-18 minutes, until soft, with a generous pinch of salt. Drain and return potatoes to pot.
  2. Use a potato masher and mash until smooth.
  3. Meanwhile, melt butter and milk in a separate saucepan until warm.
  4. Add melted butter/milk combination and sour cream to potatoes and stir.
  5. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
